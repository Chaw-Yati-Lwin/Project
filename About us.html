About Us



Our Hospital


Our Alpha Health Care Hospital was officially open in July 12th, 2006. Our hospital provides international standard health care with high quality
medical equipments and machines. All of our hospitals are open 24/7 and we're always ready if there's any emergency. Our hospital is a health
care institution providing patient treatment with specialized health science and auxiliary healthcare staff and medical equipment.




Hospital and Rooms


We have altogether 4 hospitals in different cities and the hospital located in Yangon is the main out of all. There're 150+ patient rooms in each of our hospital.
We try our best make the patients' room clean, tidy and sanitized. Not just 4 of the hospitals, we also have one clinic each open in every states and divisions.
Our clinics are open at 9:00 am and close at 6:00 pm.
